# Third-party notices

Dosty Speak is built with Qt and can optionally call external TTS engines.

## Qt

Qt is used for the GUI. Check the Qt license that applies to your distribution method.

Website: https://www.qt.io/

## eSpeak NG

On Linux, Dosty Speak can call `espeak-ng` as an external system command.

Website: https://github.com/espeak-ng/espeak-ng

## Piper TTS

Dosty Speak can install/use Piper TTS and download Piper voice models when requested by the user.

Website: https://github.com/rhasspy/piper

## Platform TTS

- macOS: `say`
- Windows: PowerShell + `System.Speech`

These are platform-provided components.
